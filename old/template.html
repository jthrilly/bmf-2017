Why We Created This Space

Over the years we have come across lots of people who resonated with some of our ideas. So we decided to find a way to continue our dialogue.

We, along with many others have been saying that not all the prevailing ideas in society contribute to fulfilment or answer or needs. For instance it has been shown that having more ‘things’ does not correlate well with fulfilment. So what does? A growing number of voices have been pointing to some answers; Mindful living, positive relationships and being part of some activity that contributes to the benefit of others seem to help us feel better about ourselves and the world.

This seems to be pointing to something essential. We want to be one small voice that contributes to this discussion. Mindful-monkey is a space to find ideas and resources, so stay in touch by subscribing to our newsletter or RSS feed.
